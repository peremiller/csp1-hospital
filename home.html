What we do

A full range of medical services

LPDH offers a wide range of health services delivered by dedicated specialists using modern medical facilities.

Emergency Medicine

Round-the-clock emergency room with rapid triage and critical-care response.

Surgery & Anesthesia

Well-equipped operating rooms for surgical and non-surgical procedures.

Pediatrics

Kid-friendly rooms and specialists who make young patients feel at ease.

OB-Gynecology

Complete obstetrics and gynecology care for mothers at every stage.

CT Scan & Imaging

Computerized tomography producing detailed cross-sectional diagnostics.

In-Patient Wards

Private and semi-private wards, well maintained with healthcard support.

Laboratory

Accurate clinical laboratory services supporting fast, reliable diagnosis.

Consultations

Dedicated consultation rooms for initial examinations and check-ups.
