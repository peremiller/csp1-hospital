Reception

This is the main reception area for the hospital. It is also referred to as foyer, lobby, or entrance hall.

CT Scan

Our computerized tomography (CT) Scan unit combines a series of X-ray images taken from different angles around the body and uses computer processing to create cross-sectional images of the bones, blood vessels, and soft tissues inside the body.

Consultation Room

Patients do initial consultation and examination with our doctors designated rooms, before they are given diagnosis or asked to undergo further medical assessment or treatment.

Operation Room

Our operation rooms are well-equipped for both surgical and non-surgical procedures. Operations are also monitored to ensure that standard medical practices are being performed well.

Semi-Private Ward

Non-VIP rooms such as semi-private wards are well maintained. Most stays in our semi-private wards are waived free of charge upon processing of their healthcard claims.

Kid-Friendly Rooms

Children are less hesitant to undergo medical assessment and treatment in our hospital, since we have created a fun atmosphere for them in-house.
