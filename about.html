About LPDH

Healthcare with heart,
since day one.

Las Piñas Doctors Hospital is an accredited 130-bed training hospital committed to compassionate, accessible, and high-quality care for every member of the community.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, accessible, high-quality, cost-effective healthcare to the community; to promote health; to educate healthcare professionals; and to participate in appropriate clinical research.

Our Vision

To be an innovative, leading regional health system dedicated to advancing the health and transforming the lives of the people we serve through excellent clinical quality; accessible, patient-centered, caring service; and unmatched physician and employee commitment.

What guides us

Our Core Values

Four principles shape every decision we make and every patient we serve.

A

Advancement

We're committed to setting ambitious goals and moving healthcare and our communities forward.

I

Inclusiveness

Everyone working together, collaboratively — patients, families, and staff alike.

R

Respect

In our regard for, and actions toward, our communities, patients, and each other.

R

Responsibility

Acting in honest, forthright, and fiscally responsible ways at all times.
